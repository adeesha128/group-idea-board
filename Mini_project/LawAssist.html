Emergency Legal Guidance

In a crisis? Know who to contact and what steps to take immediately.

🚔
If You Are Arrested
Stay calm, do not resist. You have the right to remain silent. Demand to speak with a lawyer before making any statement.
🏠
Domestic Violence
Call police (119) immediately. Seek safety first. Contact a family lawyer and women's shelter for protection orders.
💸
Workplace Exploitation
Document everything. Contact the Department of Labour. An employment lawyer can help you file a formal complaint.
🔐
Cybercrime / Fraud
Report to Sri Lanka CERT. Contact your bank immediately. A cyber law attorney can advise on civil recovery options.
